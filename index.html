About Me

Hello! I’m Sumaya Akter Lima — a student who finds joy in creativity and simple things in life. I have a deep love for crafting, where I bring small ideas to life using paper, colors, and handwork.

Alongside crafting, I enjoy cooking delicious meals, exploring my imagination through painting, and capturing quiet moments with photography. These hobbies give me peace and allow me to express myself in many different ways.
